About

OpenPlanetary started as an idea among the participants of the 2015 ESAC Planetary GIS Workshop.
The objectives and discussions of the community, so far, include:

  • Community Building

    ... to discuss and implement coordinated online solutions for the planetary science and mapping communities to share knowledge, resources, code, tools and services.

  • Use Cases

    ... for planetary scientists to discuss existing and new scientific use cases, both single- and cross-disciplinary.

  • Planetary Mapping

    ... for planetary geoscientists and cartographers to discuss and share tools, techniques and resources, including GIS.

  • Tutorials & Hacks

    ... a place to share and discuss small technical issues, the hacks that might solve them and, if need, request, organise and coordinate longer webinar/tutorials by community experts.

  • Tool Demos

    ... to discuss, and organise online demos and surveys of, existing end developping tools and services used by planetary scientists.
